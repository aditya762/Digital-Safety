We are a team of health enthusiasts who want to raise awareness about the harmful effects of electronic gadgets on our health. Our mission is to help people use electronic gadgets safely and healthily. Electronic gadgets, such as smartphones, laptops, tablets, and other digital devices, have become an integral part of our daily lives. While these gadgets provide many benefits, such as increased connectivity and convenience, they can also have negative impacts on our health.

together we can save the planet

Extended use of electronic gadgets can lead to several health problems, including eye strain, headaches, neck and back pain, and poor posture. Exposure to the blue light emitted by electronic screens can also disrupt sleep patterns and lead to insomnia.


In addition, the excessive use of electronic gadgets can contribute to sedentary behavior, which can increase the risk of obesity, diabetes, and other chronic health conditions. Furthermore, the use of electronic gadgets can interfere with social interactions and negatively impact mental health.

Prevention Tips

Take frequent breaks:

Taking regular breaks from using electronic gadgets can help prevent health problems. Aim for a 10-15 minute break every hour.

Adjust screen settings:

Adjust the brightness and contrast of your screen to reduce eye strain. Make sure your screen is at the right distance and angle from your eyes.

Reduce blue light exposure:

Blue light emitted by electronic gadgets can disrupt sleep and cause eye strain. Consider using blue light filters or wearing blue light blocking glasses.

Practice the 20-20-20 rule:

Every 20 minutes, take a break and look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds. This can help prevent eye strain.
